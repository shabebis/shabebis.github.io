Hi there! I'm Shania Cabrera.

I'm a 21-year-old information science student with a minor in game design at Cornell University. But beyond the formalities, I'm a creative thinker with a boundless passion for crafting engaging and intuitive user experiences.

My journey into UX design began with a lifelong love affair with gaming and animation. These art forms have always captivated me, and I've often found myself diving deep into the intricacies of game mechanics, character design, and storytelling. What drives me as a budding UX designer is the challenge of creating digital experiences that resonate with users on a profound level. I'm on a mission to make designs that not only look visually stunning but feel fantastic to use.

Beyond UX design, I love composing and producing music. Other times, I enjoy indulging in a dose of visual storytelling through animations. When I'm not working on UX projects, you might catch me battling demons in DOOM or grooving to Rhythm Heaven. Satoshi Kon, Gainax, Boa, and Loathe are just some of my other creative inspirations.

As I venture into the world of UX design, I'm eager to bring my strategic thinking and design sensibilities to an innovative gaming or technology company. My mission is to make technology more human-centric, ensuring that every interaction leaves a lasting impression. If you'd like to learn more about my background, please take a peek at my resume and connect with me on LinkedIn!
