Hi there! I'm Shania Cabrera.

I'm a senior at Cornell University studying Information Science (UX design) and minoring in Game Design.

My journey into UX design began with a lifelong love for gaming and animation. I've often found myself diving deep into the intricacies of game mechanics, character design, and storytelling. What drives me as a budding UX designer is the challenge of creating digital experiences that resonate with users.

Beyond UX design, I love composing and producing music. Other times, I enjoy indulging in some gaming... whether that be DOOM or Rhythm Heaven.

Satoshi Kon, Boa, and Loathe are just some of my other creative inspirations!

My mission is to make technology more human-centric, ensuring that every interaction leaves a lasting impression. If you'd like to learn more about my background, please take a peek at my resume or LinkedIn!
